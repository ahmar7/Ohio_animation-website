Office Admin / Personal Assistant / Accounting

Job Details

About The Role

  • Position: Office Admin / Personal Assistant / Accounting
  • Location: Limassol
  • Term: Full-Time
  • Schedule: Mon to Fri, 9am to 5pm with a 30min break

What We're Looking For

  • We are looking for a highly competent and motivated Office Administrator / PA to help with the daily administrative operations of our Agency in Limassol, Cyprus. 
  • The candidate should have excellent organisational skills, be proficient in MS Excel and Word, fluent in English and Greek, have experience in book-keeping, and be very capable with digital technology. 

What You Will Do

  • Office organisation and administration including document creation, management & filing, correspondence and communication, organising meetings and managing diaries, maintaining supplies, and resolving office-related issues.
  • Book-keeping including filing expenses, invoicing, making online payments, monitoring of receivables, payroll and compiling primary accounting documents to send to auditors
  • Assisting with social media and website related tasks 
  • PA duties for executives and assisting with project tasks

The Skills You Require

  • Experience in relevant administrative roles
  • Excellent command of the Greek and English Languages, both verbal and written
  • Excellent knowledge of Microsoft Excel, Word, Outlook and Adobe Acrobat
  • Excellent digital skills (social media platforms, mail clients, web browsers, communications apps) 
  • Good knowledge of bookkeeping, invoicing, online payments and payroll
  • Organisation, problem-solving, planning and time management skills
  • Business awareness and critical thinking
  • Detail-oriented, adaptable and resourceful
